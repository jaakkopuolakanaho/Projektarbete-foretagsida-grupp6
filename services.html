Pricing and Subscription

Basic Plan

$9.99/month

  • Access to a library of guided meditations (up to 50 sessions)
  • Basic progress tracking (daily streaks and session history)
  • Community access (forums and discussion boards)
  • Monthly newsletters with mindfulness tips

Ideal for individuals new to mindfulness who want to explore meditation without a large commitment.

Premium Plan

$19.99/month

  • Everything in the Basic Plan
  • Expanded library of guided meditations (up to 150 sessions)
  • AI-powered personalized meditation recommendations
  • Progress tracking with detailed analytics (mood tracking and insights)
  • Access to live group meditation sessions (monthly)
  • Exclusive articles and resources from mindfulness experts

Ideal for users looking for a more comprehensive mindfulness experience with personalized features.

Pro Plan

$29.99/month

  • Everything in the Premium Plan
  • Unlimited access to all guided meditations (over 300 sessions)
  • One-on-one coaching sessions with certified mindfulness instructors (2 sessions per month)
  • Advanced AI features, including tailored daily mindfulness reminders and habit-building tools
  • Exclusive workshops and webinars (quarterly)
  • Priority customer support

Ideal for Serious practitioners seeking a fully immersive and personalized mindfulness journey.

*Additional Notes

  • **Free Trial: Offer a 14-day free trial for all plans to allow users to explore features before committing.

This tiered pricing structure aims to cater to various user needs while providing flexibility and value as users progress on their mindfulness journey.
